My Personal Values

Integrity

  • I believe in being honest and being consistent in my actions. I strive to have strong morals everyday and will always take responsibility for my actions. I developed this value from family expectations, and from personal experiences. Without integrity, trust breaks down. Without it I may lose confidence in myself, my relationships can be damged, and I may feel disconnected from my own sense of self.

Responsibility

  • Responsibility helps me stay organized, earn trust, and manage my life effectively. It helps me succeed in school and work. Ignoring my responsibility can lead to missed opportunities, damaged trust, poor performance, and increased stress from unfinished tasks. I developed this value from family expectations, school and work experiences.
  • My parents always talked the importance of staying committed to my studies. They taught me that effort and determination lead to success so that I won't have to face the hardships that they did.

Perseverance

  • Perseverance helps me stay focused on long‑term goals, grow through adversity, and develop confidence in my ability to overcome obstacles. This value came from me overcoming difficult experiences such as studying for a hard exam, and learning from my failure such as failing a final. Without perseverance, my goals may go unfinished, I might feel overwhelmed by challenges, and I may miss opportunities for growth and success.

Discipline

  • Discipline helps me stay on track, build good habits, and achieve long‑term success. It keeps me moving forward even when things get difficult. This value came from personal goals, and experiences where my consistency led to various improvements. Without discipline, I start to procrastinate, lose focus, or I might fall behind on responsibilities. I may also take longer to reach my goals or they won't be achieved at all.
